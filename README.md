# End-to-End-Marketing-Data-Engineering-Project

A D2C beauty brand, YourBeauty Naturals, has been steadily building its presence since 2020 by launching and selling high-grade skincare, makeup, and personal care products. As part of its brand-building initiatives, the company has been running paid campaigns across Zepto, Instagram, and Google Ads from 2023 to 2025. 

Each year, campaigns were executed in two structured phases: during the first six months, three campaigns were launched—one on each platform—followed by three new campaigns across the same platforms in the next six months. This structure was consistently repeated every year. However, each platform generated raw advertising data in slightly different formats, resulting in fragmented datasets with no centralized analytics layer. 

Now in 2026, leadership aims to identify which platform delivered the highest ROI, compare viewership against actual website visits, analyze funnel drop-offs, evaluate customer acquisition efficiency, and optimize future budget allocation through a unified and structured analytics framework.
